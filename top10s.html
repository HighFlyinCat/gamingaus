Top 10's for PC

The top 10 games released on PC in 2014.

These top 10's are for games released in 2014 so far. It does not include games that are due for release in the future.

1) Kentucky Route Zero

A powerfully evocative and beautiful subversion of point-and-click rote, but occasionally opaque and disorienting.

2) Dark Souls II

More accessible, challenging, and rewarding than its predecessor, this is the Dark Souls sequel PC gamers deserve.

3) Hearthstone: Heroes of Warcraft

A fast, deep and exciting card game let down by its business model. Great when you're on a roll, rough when you're not.

4) Divinity: Original Sin

A little obtuse in places, but otherwise this is the best new RPG in years. Demands your time and your brain, but it's worth it.

5) Titanfall

The most exciting multiplayer shooter in recent years, held back from greatness by its questionable staying power.

6) South Park: The Stick of Truth

A fun, polished, handcrafted RPG attached to a genuinely funny 15-hour-long South Park episode.

7) Battleblock Theater

Charming, clever and funny, this is one of the best new platformers on the PC at the moment. Recommended.

8) The Walking Dead: In Harm's Way

Telltale's character drama is as strong as ever, but Clementine's boldest dialogue options end up making her more capable than the adults around.

9) Nidhogg

A brilliant marriage of mechanics, level design and music that will be played and talked about for years to come.

Watch Dogs

Creative hacking and covert multiplayer modes bring exciting new life to otherwise familiar open-world man-shooting.
